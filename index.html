Investment Philosophy

Why Now?

  • Rising demand for alternative yield sources among investors.
  • Strengthening regulatory frameworks are boosting investor confidence in securitization.
  • India's evolving private credit landscape offers untapped opportunities.
  • Traditional lenders focus on top-tier borrowers, leaving high-quality mid-market originators under-served.

Investment Thesis

  • High Yield: 11-14% pre-tax returns (~2x the yield of traditional fixed-income instruments).
  • Stable Income: Stable and predictable cash flows for investors.
  • Risk Protection: Robust risk framework (e.g., bankruptcy-remote structures) to safeguard capital.
  • Diversification: Multi-sector, granular portfolios to reduce concentration risk.
